Multi-Partner MedTech Programme Delivery for Paediatric Rehabilitation Cyflawni Rhaglen MedTech Aml-Bartner ar gyfer Adsefydlu Pediatrig

Coordinating an Innovate UK-funded collaboration between GripAble, Imperial College London, and NHS clinical partners to develop the SqueezAble device for children with cerebral palsy. Cydlynu cydweithrediad a ariennir gan Innovate UK rhwng GripAble, Coleg Imperial Llundain, a phartneriaid clinigol y GIG i ddatblygu'r ddyfais SqueezAble ar gyfer plant â pharlys yr ymennydd.

Context Cyd-destun

An Imperial spinout scaling smart rehabilitation Cwmni deillio Imperial yn ehangu adsefydlu clyfar

GripAble, a spinout from Imperial College London, had developed a smart handheld rehabilitation device already deployed in over 350 healthcare locations across the UK. The device was transforming how clinicians delivered upper-limb rehabilitation, combining soft-sensor technology with gamified therapy to improve patient engagement and outcomes.

Roedd GripAble, cwmni deillio o Goleg Imperial Llundain, wedi datblygu dyfais adsefydlu llaw glyfar a oedd eisoes wedi'i defnyddio mewn dros 350 o leoliadau gofal iechyd ledled y DU. Roedd y ddyfais yn trawsnewid sut roedd clinigwyr yn cyflawni adsefydlu aelodau uchaf, gan gyfuno technoleg synhwyrydd meddal â therapi wedi'i gamio i wella ymgysylltiad a chanlyniadau cleifion.

Winning an £800k Innovate UK Biomedical Catalyst grant opened the door to developing SqueezAble — a next-generation soft-sensor device designed specifically for children with cerebral palsy. But delivering a grant-funded, multi-partner R&D programme involving a startup, a world-class university lab, and NHS clinical teams required a different kind of expertise: structured programme management that could hold the complexity together.

Roedd ennill grant Catalydd Biofeddygol Innovate UK gwerth £800k yn agor y drws i ddatblygu SqueezAble — dyfais synhwyrydd meddal y genhedlaeth nesaf wedi'i chynllunio'n benodol ar gyfer plant â pharlys yr ymennydd. Ond roedd cyflawni rhaglen ymchwil a datblygu aml-bartner a ariennir gan grant yn cynnwys cwmni cychwyn, labordy prifysgol o'r radd flaenaf, a thimau clinigol y GIG yn gofyn am fath gwahanol o arbenigedd: rheoli rhaglenni strwythuredig a allai ddal y cymhlethdod at ei gilydd.

Objective Amcan

Delivering a grant-funded medtech programme on time and on track Cyflawni rhaglen medtech a ariennir gan grant ar amser ac ar y trywydd iawn

VDB was brought in to provide programme management for the SqueezAble project — coordinating delivery across GripAble's product team, Imperial College's Department of Bioengineering, and NHS clinical partners.

Daeth VDB i mewn i ddarparu rheoli rhaglenni ar gyfer prosiect SqueezAble — cydlynu cyflawni ar draws tîm cynnyrch GripAble, Adran Biobeirianeg Coleg Imperial, a phartneriaid clinigol y GIG.

The challenge was not just technical. It was about aligning different institutional cultures, managing funder expectations, and ensuring that an ambitious research programme maintained the pace and rigour needed to meet Innovate UK milestones — while keeping the end user, children with cerebral palsy, at the centre of every decision.

Nid her dechnegol yn unig oedd hon. Roedd yn ymwneud ag alinio diwylliannau sefydliadol gwahanol, rheoli disgwyliadau cyllidwyr, a sicrhau bod rhaglen ymchwil uchelgeisiol yn cynnal y cyflymder a'r trylwyredd sydd eu hangen i gwrdd â cherrig milltir Innovate UK — tra'n cadw'r defnyddiwr terfynol, plant â pharlys yr ymennydd, yng nghanol pob penderfyniad.

Holding complexity together: aligning a startup, a university lab, and NHS clinical teams around a shared programme of grant-funded R&D. Dal cymhlethdod at ei gilydd: alinio cwmni cychwyn, labordy prifysgol, a thimau clinigol y GIG o amgylch rhaglen a rennir o ymchwil a datblygu a ariennir gan grant.

What We Delivered Beth Wnaethon Ni Gyflawni

End-to-end programme delivery across three partners Cyflawni rhaglenni o'r dechrau i'r diwedd ar draws tri phartner

01

Programme & Milestone Management Rheoli Rhaglenni a Cherrig Milltir

Managed the full SqueezAble programme across GripAble, Imperial College's Department of Bioengineering, and NHS clinical partners. Tracked technical milestones, coordinated workstreams, and compiled progress reports for Innovate UK — ensuring compliance and deliverable tracking throughout the £800k grant lifecycle. Rheoli rhaglen SqueezAble lawn ar draws GripAble, Adran Biobeirianeg Coleg Imperial, a phartneriaid clinigol y GIG. Tracio cerrig milltir technegol, cydlynu ffrydiau gwaith, a llunio adroddiadau cynnydd ar gyfer Innovate UK — sicrhau cydymffurfiaeth ac olrhain cyflawniadau trwy gydol cylch oes y grant gwerth £800k.

02

Multi-Partner Coordination Cydlynu Aml-Bartner

Facilitated collaboration between Dr Firat Guder's research group (soft-sensor technology at Imperial) and GripAble's product team. Managed clinical input from Imperial College Healthcare NHS Trust and coordinated occupational therapy expertise — bridging the gap between academic research, commercial product development, and frontline clinical need. Hwyluso cydweithrediad rhwng grŵp ymchwil Dr Firat Guder (technoleg synhwyrydd meddal yn Imperial) a thîm cynnyrch GripAble. Rheoli mewnbwn clinigol o Ymddiriedolaeth GIG Gofal Iechyd Coleg Imperial a chydlynu arbenigedd therapi galwedigaethol — pontio'r bwlch rhwng ymchwil academaidd, datblygu cynnyrch masnachol, ac angen clinigol rheng flaen.

03

Funder Communications & Reporting Cyfathrebu ag Arianwyr ac Adrodd

Maintained ongoing funder relationship with Innovate UK. Prepared and submitted milestone reports, managed budget spend tracking, and handled escalation processes — keeping the programme on solid footing with its primary funding body. Cynnal perthynas barhaus â'r cyllidwr gydag Innovate UK. Paratoi a chyflwyno adroddiadau cerrig milltir, rheoli olrhain gwariant cyllideb, a thrin prosesau uwchgyfeirio — cadw'r rhaglen ar sylfaen gadarn gyda'i phrif gorff cyllido.

04

Risk & Budget Governance Llywodraethu Risg a Chyllideb

Maintained a risk register and escalation framework across the consortium. Monitored budget allocation and spend against Innovate UK requirements, ensuring that resources were deployed effectively and that any emerging risks were identified and addressed early. Cynnal cofrestr risg a fframwaith uwchgyfeirio ar draws y consortiwm. Monitro dyraniad cyllideb a gwariant yn erbyn gofynion Innovate UK, gan sicrhau bod adnoddau'n cael eu defnyddio'n effeithiol a bod unrhyw risgiau sy'n dod i'r amlwg yn cael eu nodi a'u mynd i'r afael â nhw'n gynnar.

05

Clinical Integration & User-Centred Focus Integreiddio Clinigol a Ffocws ar y Defnyddiwr

Ensured that clinical insight from NHS occupational therapists and rehabilitation specialists remained central to the development process. Coordinated user testing and clinical feedback loops so that SqueezAble was shaped by the people who would use it — therapists and children alike. Sicrhau bod mewnwelediad clinigol gan therapyddion galwedigaethol ac arbenigwyr adsefydlu'r GIG yn parhau'n ganolog i'r broses ddatblygu. Cydlynu profion defnyddwyr a dolenni adborth clinigol fel bod SqueezAble yn cael ei lunio gan y bobl a fyddai'n ei ddefnyddio — therapyddion a phlant fel ei gilydd.

Why It Mattered Pam Roedd yn Bwysig

Grant-funded medtech needs more than good science Mae medtech a ariennir gan grantiau angen mwy na gwyddoniaeth dda

Multi-partner medtech programmes carry a particular kind of risk. The science may be excellent, the clinical need proven, the funding secured — but without structured programme delivery, even the most promising collaborations can lose momentum, miss milestones, or fail to translate research into real-world impact.

Mae rhaglenni medtech aml-bartner yn cario math penodol o risg. Gall y wyddoniaeth fod yn rhagorol, yr angen clinigol wedi'i brofi, y cyllid wedi'i sicrhau — ond heb gyflawni rhaglenni strwythuredig, gall hyd yn oed y cydweithrediadau mwyaf addawol golli momentwm, methu cerrig milltir, neu fethu â throsi ymchwil yn effaith byd go iawn.

The difference between a funded project and a delivered one is programme management that holds the complexity together — across institutions, disciplines, and timelines. Y gwahaniaeth rhwng prosiect a ariennir ac un a gyflawnir yw rheoli rhaglenni sy'n dal y cymhlethdod at ei gilydd — ar draws sefydliadau, disgyblaethau, ac amserlenni.

Our work ensured that GripAble's SqueezAble programme stayed on track: milestones were met, partners remained aligned, funder requirements were satisfied, and clinical insight informed every stage. We brought the programme management discipline that allowed good science to become a viable, impactful product.

Sicrhaodd ein gwaith fod rhaglen SqueezAble GripAble yn aros ar y trywydd iawn: cwrddwyd â cherrig milltir, arhosodd partneriaid yn alinedig, bodlonwyd gofynion cyllidwyr, ac roedd mewnwelediad clinigol yn llywio pob cam. Daethâm â'r ddisgyblaeth rheoli rhaglenni a ganiataodd i wyddoniaeth dda ddod yn gynnyrch hyfyw ac effeithiol.

Outcome Canlyniad

From funded project to global medtech platform O brosiect a ariennir i lwyfan medtech byd-eang

The SqueezAble programme was delivered successfully, with all Innovate UK milestones met and the grant fully executed. But the impact went far beyond the funded project itself. GripAble went on to raise an $11M Series A round, expand into the US market through a partnership with Medline, and reach over 50% of UK stroke care pathways.

Cyflawnwyd rhaglen SqueezAble yn llwyddiannus, gyda holl gerrig milltir Innovate UK wedi'u cwrdd a'r grant wedi'i weithredu'n llawn. Ond aeth yr effaith ymhell y tu hwnt i'r prosiect a ariennir ei hun. Aeth GripAble ymlaen i godi rownd Cyfres A o $11M, ehangu i farchnad yr Unol Daleithiau trwy bartneriaeth â Medline, a chyrraedd dros 50% o lwybrau gofal strôc y DU.

The structured programme management that VDB provided during the critical early phase helped establish the operational rigour and credibility that supported GripAble's subsequent growth — from a funded research collaboration to a globally recognised rehabilitation technology company.

Helpodd y rheoli rhaglenni strwythuredig a ddarparwyd gan VDB yn ystod y cyfnod cynnar hanfodol i sefydlu'r trylwyredd gweithredol a'r hygrededd a gefnogodd dwf dilynol GripAble — o gydweithrediad ymchwil a ariennir i gwmni technoleg adsefydlu a gydnabyddir yn fyd-eang.
