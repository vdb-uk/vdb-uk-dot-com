Our Net Zero Commitment

A practical commitment to reduce our footprint and reach net zero by 2040.

Our commitment

Net zero by

2040

Ten years ahead of the UK's national target

VANDENBERGHUK Ltd is committed to achieving net zero greenhouse gas emissions by 2040, ten years ahead of the UK's national target.

We are a small, remote-first consultancy with a deliberately light footprint, and reducing it further is part of how we run the business.

Today

What we already do

  • Our office is supplied by solar power and purchases its remaining electricity on a green tariff.
  • We default to dialling into meetings rather than travelling, and travel only when it is genuinely necessary.
  • When we do travel, we choose the lowest-carbon practical option, preferring rail over car and air.
  • We run a paperless, digital-first practice, and we extend the working life of our equipment rather than replacing it on a cycle.
  • As a matter of preference, our computing and phone technology is sourced from refurbished suppliers, except where specific technology is critical to delivering a particular project.
The road to 2040

What we will do

  • Measure and record our operational emissions each year, using a recognised SME carbon calculator, so progress is tracked against a baseline rather than asserted.
  • Prefer suppliers and service providers with credible published net zero or carbon reduction commitments when we buy.
  • Keep reduction first: we will only use verified carbon removal to neutralise the small residual footprint that remains as we approach 2040.
  • Review this commitment annually and publish our progress on this page.
Beyond our footprint

Our work contributes, too

Through our consultancy work we also help clients secure funding for and deliver net zero, circular economy and resource efficiency programmes, so our largest contribution to decarbonisation is the work itself.

This commitment is owned by the Director and reviewed annually.
