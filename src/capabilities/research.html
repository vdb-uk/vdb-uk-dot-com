Research & Innovation

Evidence-based insights that validate bold ideas before a penny is spent.

Before building anything, you need to know the idea is sound. VDB's research team undertakes primary and secondary research to validate new concepts, using human-centred design methods and peer-reviewed evidence. We help organisations move from "we think this could work" to "here's the evidence it will work."

Our research informs funding bids, product strategies, and policy decisions. We've worked with Imperial College London, the University of Cambridge, and leading UK innovation hubs.
