About VDB

Who we are

Whatever the challenge, we deliver it

VDB is a multidisciplinary consultancy that spans research, funding strategy, science and engineering, ESG, and programme delivery. We don't specialise in one discipline, we assemble the right team for each brief and deliver the outcome.

Our work crosses cleantech, healthtech, fintech, government, and the cultural sector. We've unlocked over £255 million for UK R&D projects, partnering with organisations from Imperial College London and the University of Cambridge to Defra and Innovate UK.

Founder & Director

Hannah Van Den Bergh

Hannah Van Den Bergh is the Founder and Director of VDB, leading the consultancy across research strategy, funding architecture, science and engineering programmes, and ESG advisory. Her background spans sustainability research with institutions including Cambridge and Imperial, climate-policy work linked to the UNFCCC, and advisory roles across finance, industry, and government. That breadth gives VDB a distinctive ability to connect technical ambition with commercial reality.

She founded VDB to help organisations turn bold ideas into funded, credible, and scalable programmes. Under her leadership, VDB has unlocked more than £255 million for UK R&D projects, supporting start-ups, universities, corporates, and public bodies to shape stronger business models, build the right partnerships, and bring innovation to market. Hannah is known for combining strategic insight with practical delivery, helping VDB bring clarity, momentum, and trusted leadership to complex environmental and innovation challenges.
