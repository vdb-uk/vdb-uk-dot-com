About VDB Ynglŷn â VDB

Multidisciplinary consultancy delivering complex programmes across the UK. Ymgynghoriaeth amlddisgyblaeth sy'n cyflawni rhaglenni cymhleth ledled y DU.

Who we are Pwy ydym ni

Whatever the challenge, we deliver it Beth bynnag yw'r her, rydym yn ei chyflawni

VDB is a multidisciplinary consultancy that spans research, funding strategy, science and engineering, ESG, and programme delivery. We don't specialise in one discipline — we assemble the right team for each brief and deliver the outcome.

Mae VDB yn ymgynghoriaeth amlddisgyblaeth sy'n cwmpasu ymchwil, strategaeth gyllido, gwyddoniaeth a pheirianneg, ESG, a chyflawni rhaglenni. Nid un ddisgyblaeth yw ein harbenigedd — rydym yn cydosod y tîm cywir ar gyfer pob briff ac yn cyflawni'r canlyniad.

Our work crosses cleantech, healthtech, fintech, government, and the cultural sector. We've unlocked over £255 million for UK R&D projects, partnering with organisations from Imperial College London and the University of Cambridge to Defra and Innovate UK.

Mae ein gwaith yn croesi technoleg lân, technoleg iechyd, technoleg ariannol, llywodraeth, a'r sector diwylliannol. Rydym wedi datgloi dros £255 miliwn ar gyfer prosiectau ymchwil a datblygu'r DU, gan weithio gyda sefydliadau o Goleg Imperial Llundain a Phrifysgol Caergrawnt i Defra ac Innovate UK.

Founder & Director Sylfaenydd a Chyfarwyddwr

Hannah Van Den Bergh

Hannah Van Den Bergh is the Founder and Director of VDB, leading the consultancy across research strategy, funding architecture, science and engineering programmes, and ESG advisory. Her background spans sustainability research with institutions including Cambridge and Imperial, climate-policy work linked to the UNFCCC, and advisory roles across finance, industry, and government. That breadth gives VDB a distinctive ability to connect technical ambition with commercial reality.

Mae Hannah yn arwain VDB ar draws ei holl ehangder cyflawni — o strategaeth ymchwil a phensaernïaeth gyllido i raglenni gwyddoniaeth a chynghori ESG. Mae ei gwaith yn eistedd ar y croestoriad rhwng polisi, academia, a diwydiant, gan dynnu ar brofiad sy'n rhychwantu ymchwil cynaliadwyedd ym Mhrifysgol Caergrawnt, gwaith polisi hinsawdd gyda'r UNFCCC, a ymgynghori gwasanaethau ariannol.

She founded VDB to help organisations turn bold ideas into funded, credible, and scalable programmes. Under her leadership, VDB has unlocked more than £255 million for UK R&D projects, supporting start-ups, universities, corporates, and public bodies to shape stronger business models, build the right partnerships, and bring innovation to market. Hannah is known for combining strategic insight with practical delivery, helping VDB bring clarity, momentum, and trusted leadership to complex environmental and innovation challenges.

Mae hi wedi dylunio rhaglenni ar gyfer sefydliadau gan gynnwys Cyngor Celfyddydau Cymru, Julie's Bicycle, a C40, ac wedi adeiladu partneriaethau traws-sector sydd wedi ennill cydnabyddiaeth gan Wobr Earthshot, Terra Carta, Dyson, a Forbes. Heddiw mae'n cyfarwyddo ymgynghoriaeth sydd wedi datgloi dros £255 miliwn ar gyfer prosiectau ymchwil a datblygu'r DU.
